Meatball Pizza

Description

This time I'm making a deep pan version where the pizza is fried in a hot refractory pan.

A high edge on the pizza means that it is filled with extra goodies –in this case tomato sauce, cheese and homemade meatballs.

Ingredients

- Pizza Dough

  • 300g wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of dry yeast
  • 200 mL

- Topping

  • 1 can of chopped or whole tomatoes (400g)
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 2-3 branches of basil leaves
  • Mozzarella
  • Meat balls
  • Sliced onions, olives, paprika, etc

Steps

  1. Mix wheat flour with salt and dry yeast and stir together. Add enough lukewarm water until you have a soft dough, preferably a little sticky. Let the dough rest until it has doubled in size.
  2. Set the oven temp to 250°
  3. Use an immersion blender to blend the tomatoes, garlic and basil until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the pan in the hot oven for 10 minutes to warm it up.
  5. Divide the dough in half. Roll out one half into a circle larger than your pan (you want a high pizza edge). Cover the other half for now.
  6. Place the pizza dough in the hot pan. Sprinkle the bottom with some cheese, and place meatballs on top of the sauce.
  7. Put the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 7-9 minutes until the pizza bubbles.
  8. Garnish Pizza with bazil when taken out of oven.
