Lasagna

A super speedy lasagne recipe ready in just 30 minutes. Perfect for mid week meals!

  • Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 chopped small onion
  • 1 crushed garlic clove
  • 500g minced beef
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 100ml hot water
  • 150g frozen vegetables
  • 8 dried lasagne sheets
  • 150ml creme fraiche
  • 75g grated cheese

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 220c.
  • Heat the oil in a frying pan or large saucepan. Add the onion and fry for 2-3 minutes before adding the crushed garlic.
  • Turn heat up high to brown the mince as quickly as possible. Stir regularly to ensure it doesnâ€™t stick or burn.
  • Once meat has browned add chopped tomatoes and stock cube.
  • Add oregano and frozen vegetables along with water.
  • Stir until everything is well combined and continue to cook on high for 7 minutes.
  • Assemble lasagne sheets by altering layers of minced beef mixture and sheets, finishing with the lasagne sheet on top.
  • Spread the creme fraiche and greated cheese over the top.
  • Place lasagna in oven, reduce temperature to 200c and bake for 15 minutes.
  • Serve immediately with green saled or mixed vegetables.
