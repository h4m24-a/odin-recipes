Lasagna

Description

A super speedy lasagne recipe ready in just 30 minutes. Perfect for mid week meals!

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 chopped small onion
  • 1 crushed garlic clove
  • 500g minced beef
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 100ml hot water
  • 150g frozen vegetables
  • 8 dried lasagne sheets
  • 150ml creme fraiche
  • 75g grated cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220c.
  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan or large saucepan. Add the onion and fry for 2-3 minutes before adding the crushed garlic. Fry for another minute and then add the minced beef.
  3. Turn heat up high to brown the mince as quickly as possible. Stir regularly to ensure it doesnâ€™t stick or burn.
  4. Once meat has browned add chopped tomatoes and stock cube.
  5. Add oregano and frozen vegetables along with water.
  6. Stir until everything is well combined and continue to cook on high for 7 minutes.
  7. Assemble lasagne sheets by altering layers of minced beef mixture and sheets, finishing with the lasagne sheet on top.
  8. Spread the creme fraiche and greated cheese over the top.
  9. Place lasagna in oven, reduce temperature to 200c and bake for 15 minutes.
  10. Serve immediately with green saled or mixed vegetables.
