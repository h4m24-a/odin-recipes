Chicken Pasta

This Chicken Vegetable Skillet Pasta is super quick and easy to make. It uses simple ingredients for a meal the whole family will love!

  • Ingredients

  • 8 oz uncooked dried penne pasta
  • 3 tbsp olive oil divided
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken cut
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 red, yellow and orange pepper chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 medium shallots diced
  • 2 tbsp balasmic vinegar
  • 1btsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp fresh or dried parsley

Method

  • Cook penne according to the package directions. Once cooked, measure out ½ cup of the pasta water and set aside for later.
  • Add chicken to pan and cook for approximately 5 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add red, yellow, orange peppers and shallots to pan. Cook for 5 minutes.
  • Add garlic and cook for 1 minute longer.
  • Add balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, 1 tablespoon olive oil and ½ cup reserved pasta water and cooked penne pasta to pan.
  • Transfer to a large serving bowl or platter.
  • Sprinkle with fresh or dried parsley.
