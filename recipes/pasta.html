Chicken Vegetable Skillet Pasta

Description

This Chicken Vegetable Skillet Pasta is super quick and easy to make. It uses simple ingredients for a meal the whole family will love!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz uncooked dried penne pasta
  • 3 tbsp olive oil divided
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken cut
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 red, yellow and orange pepper chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 medium shallots diced
  • 2 tbsp balasmic vinegar
  • 1btsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp fresh or dried parsley

Steps

  1. Cook penne according to the package directions. Once cooked, measure out ½ cup of the pasta water and set aside for later. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add chicken to pan and cook for approximately 5 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally. until juices run clear and chicken is no longer pink.
  4. Add red, yellow, orange peppers and shallots to pan. Cook for 5 minutes, allowing peppers and shallots to brown on edges. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute longer.
  5. Add balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, 1 tablespoon olive oil and ½ cup reserved pasta water and cooked penne pasta to pan. Cook for 4 minutes.
  6. Transfer to a large serving bowl or platter.
  7. Sprinkle with fresh or dried parsley.
